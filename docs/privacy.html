Privacy Policy

This privacy policy document provides information on what, how, and why personal data or information is collected, used, and disclosed when this software is utilized. The objective of this policy is to provide an open environment between developer and users by openly stating what information is collected. By utilizing this software, you accept all the terms and conditions peculiar to Bunny. This Policy applies to this software only.

Permission and Access

For better and enhanced utilization of Bunny, the software is designed to request certain permissions. These need to be enabled for the software to work properly on your device.

We provide two separated apps to isolate the functionality of reading and writing files:

  • Bunny (Main App): The ePub reader. Requires Storage permission to open and display ebooks.
  • Bunny Service (Plugin App): Optional background service for downloads. Requires Storage and Manage all files (Android 11+).

Bunny Service Capabilities:

  • Full network access: To create network sockets (does not require sending data to the internet).
  • View network/Wi-Fi connections: To monitor connectivity status.
  • Run at startup: To initialize services after device boot.
  • Control vibration & Prevent sleep: To manage background tasks and notifications.
  • Install shortcuts: To add Home screen icons.

The information collected via these permissions is stored locally on your device. The developer has no access to this information.

Types of Data Collected

Personal Data

The developer doesn't collect personally identifiable data, except for optional opt-in crash logs.

Usage Data

The developer doesn't collect usage data.

Installation Data

The developer has no way to acquire knowledge about who has installed the software.

Crash Reports

Bunny is under active development. You may opt-in to send logs to help diagnose bugs. These logs contain no personal or identifying information and are deleted as soon as they are no longer needed.

Service Providers & Data Transfer

Bunny depends on third-party libraries. These third parties don't have access to your Personal Data. However, because these libraries are contained within the software, information may technically be processed in jurisdictions where data protection laws differ from your own.

Security & Children

This software is intended for general use with no restrictions on children (unless local laws state otherwise). While we strive to protect local data, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.
