Using Bunny

Getting Started

Once you install Bunny, you can import your existing .epub files directly from your device storage.

  1. Open the Library view.
  2. Tap the "+" or Import icon.
  3. Select your .epub file. The background service will automatically extract metadata and covers.

What is RSVP?

RSVP (Rapid Serial Visual Presentation) is a reading method where words are displayed one by one in the center of the screen. This eliminates the time spent moving your eyes from left to right, allowing you to increase your WPM (Words Per Minute) significantly.
