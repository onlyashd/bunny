Getting Started with Bunny

Bunny is designed to be simple, but the RSVP (Rapid Serial Visual Presentation) method might take a few minutes to get used to. Follow these steps to set up your library.

1

Importing your Books

Open the app and tap the Import button. Bunny requires access to your storage to read .epub files. Select any DRM-free ebook from your device.

Tutorial Screenshot: Import Screen
2

Configuring RSVP Speed

Inside the reader, you can adjust the WPM (Words Per Minute). If you are new to RSVP, we recommend starting at 250 WPM and gradually increasing it as your focus improves.

Tutorial Screenshot: WPM Settings
3

Using the Download Service

If you have the Bunny Service plugin installed, you can trigger background downloads. This keeps your reading experience smooth while large files are fetched in the background.

Tutorial Screenshot: Background Downloads

Pro Tip

Focus on the center of the screen. Try not to sub-vocalize (saying the words in your head) to reach speeds above 500 WPM!
